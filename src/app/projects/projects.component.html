Our first Featured Project: Daffini Jumpsuit

We are honored to help the poor people, especially in the countryside, by helping them resist the harsh and cold winter with a product made of plastic bottles, which also helps reduce plastic pollution.

    goals achieved :

    - one suit = 900 plastic bottles.

    - reduce plastic pollution by 0.02% per suit.

    - Comfortable winter.

    - reduce air pollution CO2 by 0.08% per suit

    - Create a job filled industry for this product
